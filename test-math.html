Currency dollar signs \$

The bank's hard rule: every currency dollar sign in stored prose is written \$. KaTeX auto-render respects backslash-escape and leaves these as literal $ at render time. If you ever see a paired-currency phrase eaten as math, the bank row violates the rule.
